Compared to Verilog-To-Routing (VTR), our experiments show geometric-mean (max) critical-path delay reductions of ~3% (~7%), ~2% (~4%), ~3% (~8%), and ~6% (~18%) for four 3D architectures: 3D CB, 3D CB-O, 3D CB-I, and 3D SB, respectively. We also achieve geometric-mean (max) routed wirelength reductions of ~1% (~3%), ~2% (~8%), < 1% (~5%), and ~5% (~10%), respectively.
